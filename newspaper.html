TOTTENVILLE WINS TITLE

Tottenville of Staten Island, led by four individual winners,
captured the team title with 155 points yesterday in the 10th annual
Public Schools Athletic League girls indoor track and field
championships at Pratt Institute in Brooklyn. South Shore of
Brooklyn was second at 132 with Truman of the Bronx third at 66.

NO PATTERN IN RULINGS ON PITCHERS' PLAY

Even before he walked into his hearing last Friday, Wade Boggs
was assured of a salary arbitration record because both he ($1.85
million) and the Boston Red Sox ($1.35 million) submitted figures
that surpassed the record $1.2 million salary Tim Raines won last
year.
